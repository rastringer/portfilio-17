Traffic Sign Classifier

A convuolutional neural network trained after much experimentation with epoch number and batch sizes to recognize images of traffic signs taken in a variety of condition with 98% accuracy. The network is based on the LeNet architecture, with image pre-processing and five convolutional layers.

Road Lanes Detection

A fun project using OpenCV's computer vision methods to recognize lane line in the road. The pipeline processes images and video of the open road and applies techniques including grayscale, Hough Transform and Canny edge detection to discern lane lines.
