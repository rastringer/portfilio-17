Traffic Sign Classifier

Trained a classifier to recognize 98% of validation images by using a convolutional neural network to train the program on thousands of images from a German traffic sign dataset. The network is based on the LeNet architecture, with image pre-processing, three convolutional layers and two fully connected layers.

Road Lanes Detection

A fun project using OpenCV's computer vision methods to recognize lane lines in the road. The pipeline processes images and video of the open road and applies techniques including grayscale, Hough Transform and Canny edge detection to discern lane lines.
